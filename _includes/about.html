Wildcat Offroad Park

A little about us

New, family friendly, 2,000 acre off-road park that will accommodate all off-road enthusiasts. We have over 100 miles of trails with the greatest sites in the Southeastern United States. We are conveniently located 2 miles from I-75 off Exit #49. You can find us at:

7800 N US Highway 25

East Bernstadt, KY 40729-6741

We have trails for the novice rider all the way up to the most experienced rider.

Park Hours:

  • Park office is open 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday and 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday.
  • Trails are open Monday through Friday 9 a.m. - Midnight. Saturday and Sunday 8 a.m. - Midnight.

The park is closed on Christmas Day only

All visitors must check in at the Park office before parking and entering the park to ride.

We ask that all of our Patrons please review our Park Waiver Form prior to visiting Wildcat Adventure Offroad Park.

Anyone under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a Parent or Legal Guardian.
